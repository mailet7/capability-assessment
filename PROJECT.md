# Capability Path Website

## Purpose

Capability Path helps parents develop capable, self-directed, adaptable human beings through a developmental framework focused on Body, Will, Mind, and Direction.

## Primary Goal

Guide visitors to take the assessment.

## Secondary Goals

- Explain the Capability Path framework
- Build trust
- Provide practical resources
- Publish Journal entries
- Grow a community of parents and educators

---

## Website Structure

- Home
- Why Capability Path
- Assessment
- Journal
- How Development Works
- Developmental Areas
  - Body
  - Will
  - Mind
  - Direction
- Resources
- About

---

## Page Purposes

### Home

Help visitors quickly understand what Capability Path is and guide them to the assessment.

### Why Capability Path

Explain why the system was created and why academics alone are not enough.

### Assessment

Help parents identify their child's next developmental area.

### Journal

Publish stories, observations, lessons, and reflections.

### How Development Works

Explain the developmental sequence:

**Body → Will → Mind → Direction**

### Developmental Areas

Provide detailed information about each developmental area.

### Resources

Provide downloadable tools, guides, and supporting materials.

### About

Share the story behind Capability Path.

---

## Core Framework

### Body

The Ability To Regulate

### Will

The Ability To Act

### Mind

The Ability To Think

### Direction

The Ability To Take Responsibility

---

## Design Principles

- Simplicity over complexity
- Clarity over cleverness
- Human development before academics
- Real-life application over theory
- Foundation before advancement
