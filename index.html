Why Capability Path

Most education focuses on schoolwork. Life asks for more.

Reading and math matter. But children also need to regulate their bodies, start what needs to be started, think clearly, recover from difficulty, and take responsibility.

Capability Path gives parents a way to see those foundations clearly, without turning childhood into a test.

The four developmental areas

Capability grows through the whole child.

BODY

Regulate & Care

Routines, recovery, emotions, energy, and self-care.

WILL

Act

Starting, staying, returning, and finishing.

MIND

Think & Learn

Focus, understanding, problem solving, and learning.

DIRECTION

Self-Direct

Ownership, responsibility, initiative, and wise freedom.
