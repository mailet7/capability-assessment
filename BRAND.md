# Capability Path Brand Guide

## Brand Name

Capability Path

## Tagline

Education for Real Life

---

## Mission

Help parents develop capable, self-directed, adaptable human beings.

---

## Core Idea

Most education focuses primarily on academics.

Capability Path focuses on developing the whole human being.

The goal is not merely academic achievement.

The goal is capability.

---

## Core Framework

1. Body
2. Will
3. Mind
4. Direction

### Body

The Ability To Regulate

### Will

The Ability To Act

### Mind

The Ability To Think

### Direction

The Ability To Take Responsibility

---

## Core Principle

Complexity through simplicity.

Build the foundation first.

Add complexity only when the foundation becomes stable.

---

## Audience

### Primary

- Parents
- Homeschool families

### Secondary

- Educators
- Micro-schools
- Schools

---

## Personality

- Grounded
- Calm
- Clear
- Trustworthy
- Practical
- Hopeful
- Human
- Confident

### Avoid

- Corporate
- Trendy
- Flashy
- Fear-based
- Academic-sounding

---

## Design Direction

### Desired Feel

- Simple
- Clean
- Spacious
- Natural
- Easy to read
- Professional without feeling corporate

### Avoid

- Educational clip art
- Cartoon school imagery
- Excessive animations
- Visual clutter
- Corporate stock-photo feel

---

## Writing Style

Write like a thoughtful parent sharing something important.

### Use

- Short paragraphs
- Simple language
- Real stories
- Practical observations
- Clear examples

### Avoid

- Jargon
- Academic language
- Corporate language
- Marketing language
- Overly persuasive sales copy

### Structure

Story first.

Principle second.

Application third.

The reader should discover the lesson through the story.

---

## What Capability Path Is

- A developmental framework
- A field guide for human development
- A practical operating system for families
- A path toward capability

---

## What Capability Path Is Not

- A curriculum
- A behavior management program
- A productivity system
- A parenting hack
- A replacement for parenting

---

## Desired Reader Response

"I finally have a clear path."

"I know what to focus on next."

"I understand my child better."

"I feel less overwhelmed."

"I can do this."

---

## Key Message

Children deserve more than academic completion.

They deserve the best chance of success in real life.

Capability Path exists to help families develop capable, self-directed, adaptable human beings.

Education should develop the whole human being for the whole of life.
