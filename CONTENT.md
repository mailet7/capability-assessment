# Content Rules

## Every Article Should

- Start with a story, observation, or experience
- Reveal a deeper principle
- End with practical application
- Feel hopeful
- Reduce overwhelm
- Increase clarity

## Avoid

- Academic language
- Corporate language
- Fear-based messaging
- Marketing hype
- Overcomplicated explanations
